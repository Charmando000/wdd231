Pixel & Story

Site Name

Pixel & Story represents the combination of video games as a visual medium and interactive storytelling.

Site Purpose

The site provides information about video games and interactive story-based experiences, including game details, genres, and dynamic content.

Scenarios

  • What video games offer strong storytelling and immersive experiences?
  • How can I explore and compare different video games by genre or platform?

Color Scheme

  • #1e1e2f – Header, footer, background
  • #f5f5f5 – Content background
  • #ff9800 – Accents and highlights

Typography

Montserrat is used for headings.

Roboto is used for body text.

Wireframe

Wireframes for mobile and desktop views will define the layout of the home page, including header, navigation, content sections, and footer.
